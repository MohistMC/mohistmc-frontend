#### Basic Usage:
`/shows [sound|entitys|blockentitys]`

#### Permission:
`youer.command.shows`

### -> sound
`/shows sound`
View all available sound effects
<img src="/img/docs/shows_sound.png" alt="Sound List" />
Click on the corresponding note block to play, where the note block's name is the sound effect enum available to plugins

### -> entitys
`/shows entitys`
View all entities in the current world
<img src="/img/docs/shows_entity.png" alt="Entity List" />
Click on the corresponding item to teleport to the chunk with the most entities

### -> blockentitys
`/shows blockentitys`
View all block entities with ticks in the current world
<img src="/img/docs/shows_blockentity.png" alt="Block Entity List" />
Click on the corresponding item to teleport to the chunk with the most block entities
