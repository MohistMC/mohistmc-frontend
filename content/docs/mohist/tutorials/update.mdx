# Update

How to update Mohist?

## First Method

Go to our [website](https://mohistmc.com/download/mohist) for your Minecraft version, and **replace** the **old version provided by the version you downloaded**

## Second Method

Set `check_update_auto_download` to `true` in `mohist-config/mohist.yml` then start your server, and it will automatically update.

## Third Method

Use the `/updatemohist` command and restart your server, that's it!
